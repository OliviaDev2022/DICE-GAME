GAME RULES

The dice game has two players, playing in round. In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he wishes.

Each result get added to his CURRENT score; but if the player rolls a 1, all his CURRENT score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.

Each player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his CURRENT score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn.

The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game !
